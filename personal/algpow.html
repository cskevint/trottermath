AlgPoW Problems

     Since August of 1999, I (Terry) have had the pleasure to work with the famous website called The Math Forum, and more specifically the Problem of the Week section of that site. My attention was mainly focussed on the Algebra portion of that project, a.k.a "algpow.

     This page of WTM is merely a bibliographical listing of the problems in algpow that I have been involved on the creative side of things. As I was not the inventor of all the problems in the project, I have chosen to separate the majority of them into groups, according to my involvement in the process. Group I, for example, consists of those problems that I did in fact write. Group II, on the other hand, contains items which were adapted from problems found in other sources. I took the problem from some source then re-wrote it in a different form. (Where possible, I state the source of the problem's inspiration.)

     Group III gives problems which involved the cooperation to some significant degree of other individuals. When possible, they will be likewise indentified. Finally, Group IV has a few problems that were borrowed from another source or written by someone else, not associated with the Math Forum staff.
