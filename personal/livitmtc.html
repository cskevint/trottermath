To: geomomo, grillyx, ï¿½lle the Saxon, Indiagonal Jones, King Michael, Malcolm, & bdk1

From: jestone

Hey guys! Here is my newest proposal to you all for a tournament of loop chess, chapionship variety.

  1. We'll have 8 players this time, this means each of us will play 7 games. I've chosen the top 4 from the first group that played and the top 3-plus-me from the second group. This obviously means that color assignments can't be equal. But I have consulted my USCF Official Rules of Chess handbook for how to handle this in a manner that is normally done in the chess world. See below.

  2. The player with white is primarily responsible for sending the invitation, though either person may do so.

  3. As to time limit for moves, I'd like to set my personal games at 9 days/move. That's to protect me in case of sickness or unforeseen troubles. However, those who know me, as you all do, know that I tend to move at a regular, much faster pace. So, perhaps each of you could decide what is appropriate for you. Also, set all games for public view and subject to being rated by brainking's system.

  4. As each game is finished, I would like for each player to drop me a note, telling me what happened. You can do this by brainking's message service or by dropping me an email. However, I will, as time & opportunity permits, go to your profile and see how things are going as well. I will then record the results in the cross-table above.

  5. I feel that we could make the official start for Wednesday, April 21, 2004.

  6. If there is anything I've forgotten to mention above, just ask me.

Terry [ trottermath@gmail.com ]
