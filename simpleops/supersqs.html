	Here are four squares full of numbers.  We will use them to
perform a little math magic.  Just follow the rules as given for each
square.


 1. Choose any number in the square
    that you like.  Draw a circle
    around it.

 2. Cross out all the other numbers
    that are in the same row as the
    number you chose.  Also do this
    for all the other numbers that
    are in the same column.  These
    numbers are now "gone".

 3. Repeat steps 1 & 2 until all
    numbers are either circled or
    crossed out.

 4. Add the circled numbers from
    the square.

 5. Find the letter in the alphabet
    that corresponds with the sum in
    step 4.  Let A = 1, B = 2, etc.
