A Trip to the Beach

     The Periwinkle family likes to go to the beach whenever possible. Recently during Labor Day they left their house early to enjoy some fun in the sun. At 7:05, they were one-third of the way there. Then at 7:25 they had traveled three-fourths of their drive.

     What time did the family leave their house?

     Extra: If the distance to the beach was 32 miles, what was their average rate or speed of their automobile?
